# 📊 Olist E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Olist’s Brazilian e-commerce dataset to extract insights on customer behavior, revenue trends, product performance, delivery efficiency, and payment patterns.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into interactive business intelligence dashboards to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The e-commerce platform lacked consolidated visibility into:

- Revenue distribution across states
- Product category profitability
- Customer review trends
- Delivery performance
- Payment behavior patterns

Without structured analytics, identifying high-performing segments and operational bottlenecks was difficult.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

- SQL (Data Extraction & Aggregation)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
- Data Modeling & KPI Development

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- Dataset: Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset
- Records: 100K+ transactions
- Data Includes:
  - Orders
  - Customers
  - Payments
  - Reviews
  - Products
  - Delivery details

---

## 📊 Key Dashboard Insights

### 📍 State-wise Revenue Distribution
- Identified top revenue-generating states
- Regional concentration analysis

### 📦 Top Product Categories by Profit
- Movies & Decor and IT Accessories emerged as highest contributors
- Clear profit gap between top 3 and remaining categories

### ⭐ Rating Analysis
- High ratings dominate across categories
- Identified low-rating segments needing improvement

### 💳 Payment Behavior
- Credit card accounts for majority of revenue (~12.5M)
- Debit and voucher usage significantly lower

### 🚚 Delivery Performance
- Majority deliveries completed within optimized time window
- Identified delayed delivery tail distribution

### 📆 Profit by Day
- Tuesday and Monday showed highest profit
- Weekend slightly lower revenue compared to weekdays

---

## 📈 Key Business Impact

- Improved KPI visibility across 6 major business dimensions
- Identified high-value customer and product segments
- Highlighted operational inefficiencies in delivery timelines
- Enabled strategic focus on dominant payment channel
- Provided structured framework for performance monitoring
